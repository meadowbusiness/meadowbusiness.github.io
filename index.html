About the app

Discover a new way of becoming a model. The Model App connects models and model agencies worldwide. Create your profile and let agencies find you! We have verified agencies using our app to scout new faces every day.


How it works for models!
1. Sign Up or Login using your social media account
2. Upload photos of your face, profile and body
3. Update your measurements and complete your profile
4. Your account starts with a 7-day free trial. During the free trial, 3 model agencies can see your profile. To unlock all model agencies you'll need to purchase a subscription.
5. Model agencies use our app on a daily basis. When they see and like your profile, they'll get in touch with you directly via the app.

Please note, scouting may take a while and is entirely up to the model agencies to decide who they choose to contact.


How it works for agencies!
1. Sign Up using your business email address
2. Complete your profile
3. Your profile will be verified upon submission by a member of our team, if we need more information we'll contact you.
4. Once your profile has been approved you can start scouting models. You'll have full access to the advanced filtering, all available model profiles, direct chat functionality and much more.
